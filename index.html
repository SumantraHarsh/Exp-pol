Types of Pollution

There are several types of pollution, including air pollution, water pollution, and land pollution.

Effects of Pollution

Pollution has serious effects on the environment and human health. It can lead to respiratory problems, waterborne diseases, and more.

Solutions to Pollution

We can combat pollution by reducing waste, recycling, using renewable energy, and promoting sustainable practices.
